About Us

Our Mission

We want to give secondary school and university students in the region, and beyond, an opportunity to learn more about international relations, public speaking, and current socio-political issues. We aim to promote international cooperation, international law and the role of international organisations in maintaining peace and stability in this time of regional uncertainty

Our Team

We are a student-led team with extensive MUN experence and diverse perspectives. Our goal from the beginning was to organise a conference that we would love to attend and give other ambitious youth in Moldova, as well as around the world, an amazing and accessible opportunity. In order to achieve that, we are cooperating closely with JEF Moldova and experienced MUN professionals.
