Our Mission

We want to give secondary school and university students in the region, and beyond, an opportunity to learn more about international relations, public speaking, and current socio-political issues. We aim to promote international cooperation, international law and the role of international organisations in maintaining peace and stability in this time of regional uncertainty

Our Team

This conference is student-led, organised by the Centre of Eastern European Dialogue (CEED) with the support of local and international organisations.
