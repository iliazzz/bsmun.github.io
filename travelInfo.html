Travel Information

Transport in the City

Chisinau does not have Uber, so we strongly recommend installing the Bolt or Letz apps if you wish to take taxis. Public transport is also available in order to get to the Conference venues and from the airport to the city center, however it does not function during the night (from 23:00 to 6:00). If you wish to use public transport, we recommend using Google Maps for routes and the EasyWay app for timetables and live tracking of the busses. Beware of the fact that more often than not, you will not be able to use your card to pay for a ticket. The price one journey on any kind of public transport is 6 MDL (0.3 EUR), and you will be expected to pay cash (or tap the card reader, should there be one) directly to the ticket person. Hence, if you plan on using public transport be sure to carry some cash with you.
