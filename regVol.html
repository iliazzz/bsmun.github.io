Opportunities

By volunteering at the Black Sea Model United Nations conference you will be able to gain valuable insight into the back-end workings of event planning and organisation. You will also be working alongside many motivated and talented peers who share your values, as well as closely interacting with real experts of the diplomacy world. This way, you can learn from established professionals, network and make new friends while helping out the local community and facilitating an amazing event!

Requirements

It is strongly recommended to have a solid grasp of the English language in order to be able to help our attendees. However, we welcome all applicants regardless! An extra pair of hands will be a great help no matter what your qualifications are, and we look forward to making the best of whatever skills our team brings to the table :)
