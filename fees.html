Delegate Fees

The delegate fee is €80. This fee includes three and a half days of conference, lunch on the three full days, snacks throughout the day and access to the social event.

Moldovan residents studying in public school/university will receive a discount of 50%

Chair and Vice-chair Fees

Chairs and Vice-chairs are not required to pay any fee to be a part of our conference. Food on the three full conference days, snacks and access to the social event will be provided free of charge.

Advisor Fees

The advisor fee is €80. This fee includes access to the conference space on all days, lunch on the three full days, snacks throughout the day and access to the social event. (for teachers/adults accompanying school groups)

Scholarships

At Black Sea MUN we do not believe that financial limitations should be a barrier to educational opportunities. For this reason we are excited to offer 1 full and 3 partial scholarships to our conference. All scholarship recipients will have their delegate fee waived and the recipient of the full scholarship will additionally have their cost of accommodation covered. These scholarships are for both divisions of the conference.

Eligibility and Selection Criteria

Eligibility: any student eligible to attend the conference is also eligible for a scholarship.

Selection criteria: the applications are reviewed holistically and on a case-by-case basis, taking into consideration both academic merit and demonstrated financial need. As part of the application students will have to submit a motivation letter (up to 500 words in length) detailing why they wish to attend the conference, how it will help them achieve their future academic and/or professional goals or why it will otherwise help them, and what they will bring to the conference. Additionally, the applicants will be required to submit school transcript, their CV and any relevant Model UN experience, as well as records showing financial need, if applicable.
