Eligibility Guidelines

  1. 1. Age

    • Junior Division:
      Applicants must be between the ages of 12 and 16 inclusive at the time of the conference and be enrolled in a school.
    • Senior Division:
      Applicants must be between the ages of 16 and 20 inclusive at the time of the conference and be enrolled in a school or university

  2. 2. English Proficiency

    • For both divisions applicants must have a strong command of the English language (a minimum level of C1 in CEFRL or equivalent). Proof of English competency will be required as part of the application. This can be in any form including, but not limited to, any certificate of English language proficiency, having studied at an English-taught school, having taken English classes at a non-English-taught school or outside of school, etc.

  3. 3. Prior Experience

    • We do not require delegates to have any previous Model UN experience, however experience may affect the committee and country allocations.
